Privacy Policy for Yiyo Research

Effective Date: 7/5/2024

Thank you for using Yiyo Research. As the privacy rights are fundamental rights valuable to our visitors (“you”, “your” or “yours”) in corner of the world, we treat any information that relates to you as high importance. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how we collect, use, disclose, safeguard and dispose your information when you interact with our user research and/or other activities. We encourage you to read this Policy and know your privacy rights when interacting with us.

1. Introduction

Yiyo Research ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to respecting and protecting your rights of privacy. This Privacy Policy informs you about the types of personal information (“information” or “data”) and how we access, collect, transfer and processing it, as well as your choices regarding your personal information. Your involving and participating in our user research and/or other activities will mean that you accept and agree to the provisions as described in this Policy.

2. Types of Data We Collected

When you participate in our user research and/or other activities, we may collect the types of personal information, including:

  • Contact Information:  Name, location, government ID, email address, phone number or other contact information.

  • Demographic Information: Age, gender, occupation, usage habits, interests.

  • Gaming Preferences: Information related to your gaming habits and preferences.

  • Device Information: Type of your device, the operating system version of your device, unique device identifiers.

  • Other Information You Provide to Us: Details such as the content of your communications with us. 

3. Methods of Collection

We collect personal information directly from you when you voluntarily provide it to us through interacting with our devices, surveys, interviews, and/or other research activities and services. You are not required to provide the personal information as we have requested. However, it may not be able to involve you into the interaction with our devices, surveys, interviews, and/or other research activities.

We may also collect information (including personal information) automatically through cookies and/or other similar technologies when you visit our website.

4. Purpose and Usage of Data

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • Conducting and Improving our User Research: Analyzing user’s feedback and behavior from our interviews and/or user research;  understanding user behavior and preferences to improve our gaming design and experiences; improving our research methodologies and services.

  • Communication: To communicate with you about our research activities and updates, providing other relevant information, or requesting information or feedback.

  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations subject to applicable laws. 

5. Data Sharing

We may engage third parties to act as our service providers to process the  aggregated and anonymized data relating your information for research and statistical purposes.

Our service providers will act in compliance with this Policy and our instructions strictly. They are not allowed to use the data we shared for their own purposes and must delete or return such data upon our request and instruction.

6. Data Transfer

The user research and/or other services conducted by us, may offer connection to you from the place around the world. We strictly comply with laws and regulations on the transfer of your information between countries/regions to ensure your data is protected, wherever it may be.

7. Data Disclosure

We will not disclose your information that we collect to third parties except as described in this Policy.

We may reserve the right to preserve and disclose your information that we believe, in good faith, is appropriate or necessary to (i) comply with applicable laws, (ii) be in response to judicial or governmental orders or requests, (iii) protect to our property or legal rights subject to the applicable laws.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for the purpose stated in this Policy. We aim to retain your information only for the period necessary to fulfil our obligations under this Policy, unless otherwise required or permitted by law. We will carefully assess the need, the type of data collected, relevant legal framework and limitations when determining the retention period.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

We will also implement additional protections to keep Children’s (referring to individual under the age of 13) personal information safe.

10. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and Request: Access your personal information and request corrections, updates, or deletions.

We process your personal information based on your consent and our legitimate interests in conducting user research and improving our services.

This Privacy Policy is intended to be transparent and compliant with relevant data protection laws.
